Welcome to Hillwood Cottage Terrace! We are passionate about providing our customers with a wide range of high-quality fruit trees that are perfect for any garden.

Our selection includes a diverse array of fruit trees, such as apples, pears, plums, cherries, nectarines, peaches, apricots, berries, and nuts. We specialize in propagating both heritage and modern cultivars, ensuring that our customers have access to a variety of colours, sizes, flavours, and textures.

At Hillwood Cottage Terrace, we believe that growing your own fruit is a rewarding and fulfilling experience. There's nothing quite like picking fresh, juicy fruit straight from the tree and enjoying it with your loved ones. That's why we're committed to helping our customers create beautiful and productive orchards that provide them with fresh, healthy produce throughout the year.

We offer a range of tree sizes and rootstocks to suit any garden, from small urban balconies to large rural estates. Our team of experts is always available to provide advice and guidance, whether you're an experienced gardener or a first-time fruit tree grower.

Please note that our trees are bare rooted, and we typically ship in the winter months, between mid-July and August. For tips on growing and caring for your fruit trees, be sure to check out our resources section. And if you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to get in touch.

Thank you for choosing Hillwood Cottage Terrace, and we look forward to helping you create the orchard of your dreams!
